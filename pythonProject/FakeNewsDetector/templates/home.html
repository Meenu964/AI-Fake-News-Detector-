💡 About Project

This AI-powered Fake News Detection System uses Machine Learning, TF-IDF Vectorization, Logistic Regression, the Django Framework, and advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to accurately evaluate whether a news article is Real or Fabricated. The application also extracts context sentiments and records previous query metrics for auditing.
